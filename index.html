I received my BEng degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2010. I finished my PhD in August 2014 at the University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Dr. Hubert Chan, with whom I worked as a postdoctoral fellow from 2014 to 2015. I worked as a postdoctoral researcher with Dr. Danupon Nanongkai at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden, from 2015 to 2016 before joining Huawei Noah's Ark Lab.
